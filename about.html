Quick Biography

My artistic journey started in 2018 with the exploration of several art genres and techniques, as I was trying to find out what inspired me. I began studying abstract geometry and especially fractals, then I explored 3D modelisation and image processing. Lately I have been fascinated by the newest AI-based creative techniques.
